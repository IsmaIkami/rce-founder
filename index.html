RCE (Relational Cognitive Engine)

Deterministic AI that explains every decision. EU AI Act compliant by architecture.

The Problem

Current AI systems face critical challenges that block enterprise adoption:

  • Regulatory non-compliance — EU AI Act mandates explainability for high-risk AI. Traditional LLMs cannot provide it. They are probabilistic black boxes.
  • Hallucinations — LLMs generate false information with confidence. No deterministic reasoning path means no reliability guarantee.
  • Massive energy consumption — Transformer models require enormous computational resources. Training and inference costs are unsustainable at scale.
  • Zero transparency — Enterprises cannot audit AI decisions. Attention mechanisms provide no traceable reasoning path.

Market timing: EU AI Act enforcement begins now. Regulated enterprises must deploy explainable AI or face penalties. Energy costs and hallucination risks are accelerating demand for alternatives.

The Solution

RCE replaces attention mechanisms with deterministic coherence graphs. Every inference is fully traceable. Every output is explainable. Regulatory compliance is native to the architecture.

  • Plug into existing pipelines — RCE integrates via standard APIs into any AI system. No infrastructure rewrite needed. Reduces hallucinations immediately.
  • Complete explainability — Every decision traces through the coherence graph. No post-hoc interpretation. The architecture is inherently transparent.
  • Deterministic reasoning — Same input always produces same output with identical reasoning path. Eliminates hallucinations and unpredictability.
  • Drastically lower energy footprint — Coherence graphs require fraction of compute vs. transformers. Sustainable AI at scale.
  • Regulatory compliance by design — EU AI Act compliant without modifications. Creates structural moat as regulations tighten globally.
  • Empirically validated performance — Peer-reviewed research demonstrates competitive performance with full transparency. No capability tradeoff.
