The Problem

Traditional LLMs are Black Boxes

Transformer-based models rely on probabilistic attention mechanisms that cannot explain their reasoning. This creates fundamental compliance challenges with regulations like the EU AI Act, which mandate explainability for high-risk AI systems.

Enterprises need AI that can both perform and explain—not choose between the two.

The Solution

Deterministic Coherence Graphs

RCE-LLM represents a paradigm shift in language model architecture. Instead of probabilistic attention, we use deterministic coherence graphs that model concept relationships explicitly.

Every decision is traceable. Every inference is explainable. Compliance is built into the architecture, not bolted on after.

Native Explainability

Every decision traces back through the coherence graph, providing complete transparency into model reasoning.

EU AI Act Compliant

Deterministic architecture meets regulatory requirements by design, not through post-hoc interpretation layers.

Empirically Validated

Peer-reviewed research demonstrates performance improvements while achieving full explainability.

The Opportunity

EU AI Act enforcement is accelerating. Enterprises face mounting pressure to deploy compliant AI systems. RCE-LLM solves the explainability problem while maintaining competitive performance—a combination the market desperately needs.
