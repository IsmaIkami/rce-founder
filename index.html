RCE-LLM

Deterministic language models that explain themselves. Built for EU AI Act compliance.

Traditional LLMs are black boxes. They can't explain their decisions. That's a dealbreaker for regulated industries under the EU AI Act.

RCE-LLM replaces probabilistic attention mechanisms with deterministic coherence graphs. Every inference is traceable. Every decision is explainable. Compliance is built into the architecture.

The market opportunity: EU AI Act enforcement starts now. Enterprises need compliant AI systems that actually work. We provide both.

Why This Works

  • Native explainability — Not a post-hoc hack. The architecture is deterministic by design.
  • Empirically validated — Peer-reviewed research shows performance parity with traditional models.
  • Regulatory moat — EU AI Act compliance is increasingly mandatory, creating natural customer pull.
  • Enterprise experience — 20+ years at Fortune 500 companies. Deep understanding of compliance requirements.
