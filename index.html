RCE (Relational Cognitive Engine)

Deterministic AI that explains every decision. EU AI Act compliant by architecture.

The EU AI Act requires explainability for high-risk AI systems. Traditional LLMs cannot provide it. They are probabilistic black boxes that cannot trace their reasoning.

RCE replaces attention mechanisms with deterministic coherence graphs. Every inference is fully traceable. Every output is explainable. Regulatory compliance is native to the architecture.

Market timing: EU AI Act enforcement begins now. Regulated enterprises must deploy explainable AI or face penalties. RCE is the first architecture that delivers both performance and compliance.

Core Technology Advantages

  • Complete explainability — Every decision traces through the coherence graph. No post-hoc interpretation needed. The architecture is inherently transparent.
  • Deterministic reasoning — Same input always produces same output with identical reasoning path. Eliminates AI hallucinations and unpredictability.
  • Regulatory compliance by design — EU AI Act mandates explainability. RCE provides it natively, creating a structural moat as regulations tighten.
  • Enterprise-grade performance — Empirically validated to match transformer performance while providing full transparency. No tradeoff between capability and compliance.
  • Rapid integration — Standard API compatibility. Enterprises can adopt RCE without rewriting existing systems.
